# BridgeAI

Helping people navigate community resources with AI.

## Overview

BridgeAI is an AI-powered assistant designed to help people understand what support may be available to them and what steps they can take next.

The goal is simple: turn confusing resource searches into clear, personalized action plans.

## Problem

People who need help with food, housing, employment, healthcare, or benefits often have to search across multiple websites, forms, and organizations. This can be overwhelming, especially during stressful moments.

## Solution

BridgeAI asks users about their situation and location, then generates a clear action plan with possible resource categories, documents to gather, and recommended next steps.

## Target User

BridgeAI is designed for anyone looking for community resources, especially people facing urgent life challenges such as job loss, food insecurity, housing instability, or lack of healthcare access.

## Core Features

- AI-guided intake conversation
- City/state-based resource guidance
- Personalized action plan
- Document checklist
- “Next best step” recommendation
- Simple, accessible interface
